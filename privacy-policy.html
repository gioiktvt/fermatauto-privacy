Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how mirone ("I", "me", or "the developer") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use the FermataAuto mobile application (the "App") on Android devices.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App requests the following device permissions to provide its features:

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) — Used solely for voice search and speech-to-text input within the App. Audio is processed locally on your device or forwarded to the speech recognition service you select (e.g., Google Speech Recognition). Audio recordings are not stored permanently or transmitted to the developer's servers.
  • Storage / Media files (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_AUDIO, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO, ACCESS_MEDIA_LOCATION) — Used to browse and play audio and video files stored on your device. No files are uploaded or shared externally.
  • Internet access (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, ACCESS_WIFI_STATE) — Used to stream online media content, fetch metadata, and check network connectivity.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — Used to display media playback controls in the notification shade.
  • Phone state (READ_PHONE_STATE) — Used to automatically pause playback when a phone call is received, and resume afterward.
  • Foreground service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK) — Required to keep media playback running while the App is in the background or the screen is off.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected through these permissions is used exclusively to operate the App's core features. Specifically:

  • Voice data from the microphone is used only during active voice search sessions.
  • Media files are accessed only when you browse or play them within the App.
  • No personal data is collected, stored on external servers, or sold to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services — Privacy Policy
  • Google Speech Recognition (if used for voice search) — Privacy Policy
  • ExoPlayer / media decoders — Open-source library; no data collection.

I do not control and am not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

4. Data Retention

The App does not retain any personal data on external servers. Any temporary data processed during a session (such as voice input) is discarded immediately after use.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact me so I can take appropriate action.

6. Security

I implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.
